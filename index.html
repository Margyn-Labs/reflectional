Security & trust

Your books stay yours.

Margyn reads your data to build the score. It never trades it, never sells it, and never shares it without your explicit, revocable consent.

Read-only, always

Bank and GST connections use RBI Account Aggregator and GSP rails in read-only mode — Margyn can see your data, never move it.

DPDPA-aligned by design

Every data-sharing action — including any future lender introduction — is opt-in, logged, and revocable in line with the DPDPA, 2023.

Not a lending marketplace

Margyn isn't a broker. Your verified data is a credential you can choose to use — capital access is an outcome you control, not a service sold around you.
